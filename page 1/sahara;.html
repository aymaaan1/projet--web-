Overview

The Jewel of Morocco South

The Moroccan Sahara is a world unto itself. From the towering amber dunes of Erg Chebbi near Merzouga to the vast wilderness of Erg Chigaga near Mhamid and the rising unexpectedly from the turqoise waters of the lagoon ,the White Dune of Dakhla is one of the most surreal natural wounders . So this is where Morocco reveals its most profound beauty.

Our curated journeys take you beyond the tourist trail. Sleep in luxury camps draped in Hassani textiles, ride camels across dunes at golden hour, and dine under a canopy of stars.

Morocco’s Sahara offers dramatic ergs, ancient fossil beds, hidden oases, and nomadic communities whose culture stretches back centuries.

Experiences

Saharan Moments You will Never Forget

Camel Trekking

Ride dromedaries across the dunes at golden hour. Our guides know routes untouched by crowds — paths that lead to silence and panoramic beauty.

Desert Stargazing

With zero light pollution, the Saharan night sky is among the clearest on Earth. Astronomer-guides map constellations over mint tea by the fire.

Luxury Camp Nights

Handcrafted Berber tents with real beds, carpets, and en-suite facilities. Candle-lit dinners under the stars featuring tagine and couscous.

Sandboarding

Ride the dunes — from gentle slopes for beginners to thrilling 150-meter descents down the face of Erg Chebbi.

Gnaoua Music Night

Traditional Gnaoua music performed by local musicians around the campfire — hypnotic rhythms echoing across the Sahara for centuries.

Kasbah Excursions

Visit ancient fortified kasbahs along the Route of a Thousand Kasbahs — earthen architecture telling the story of Saharan trade routes.

Sample Itinerary

Three Days in the Golden Sands

Day one — Arrival

Marrakech to the Desert Gate

Depart by private 4×4 through the Tizi n’Tichka pass. Stop at Ait Benhaddou, a UNESCO kasbah. Continue through the Draa Valley to your luxury camp. Welcome mint tea and sunset camel ride.

Day two — Immersion

Deep Into the Dunes

Sunrise from the highest dune. Morning 4×4 excursion to nomadic families and a hidden oasis. Afternoon sandboarding or meditation. Evening Gnaoua music, dinner, and stargazing.

Day three — Return

Desert to City

Final sunrise. Berber breakfast with msemen and amlou. Return via Ouarzazate with a stop at the Atlas Film Studios. Arrive in Marrakech by evening.
